How Circuit IQ Works

Our streamlined process takes you from concept to production-ready PCB design in just a few steps.

Describe Your Requirements

Specify your circuit requirements in natural language. Include details about components, connections, power needs, and design constraints.

AI-Generated Design

Our AI analyzes your requirements and generates an optimized PCB design, complete with component placement and routing.

Review & Refine

Review the generated design and provide feedback in natural language. Our AI will refine the design based on your comments.

Export & Manufacture

Export your finalized design in industry-standard formats ready for manufacturing or further refinement in your preferred CAD tool.
